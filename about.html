What does EQ Schools do?

EQ Schools provides emotional intelligence training to organizations and schools. Our workshops lead participants to gain creative confidence, to improve their self-regulation and management, to collaborate with others more effectively and to feel happier.

We deliver various training programs: Workshops, TEDx Talks, Keynote Presentations, and Mastery Courses. Every training addresses employee well-being, mindfulness, and leadership tactics so you can empower your business, your employees, and yourself.

Why It's Important To You

Research shows that emotional intelligence leads to tremendous benefits in personal and professional life. It predicts career success more than IQ or experience. Employees with high emotional intelligence are better leaders, managers, negotiators, sales people, and are more perseverant, charismatic, optimistic, and resilient.

About the Founder

Roni Habib is the founder of the EQ Institute and teaches Positive Psychology at Gunn High School in Palo Alto, California. He leads Emotional Intelligence workshops and presents about the topic in conferences, schools, and companies. He is the Chief Happiness Officer of the Ed-Tech Team, a Google Certified Teacher and works as consultant for Ed-Tech companies. Roni lived in Israel and Belgium before coming to the U.S. where he got his Masters of Education and teaching credential at Harvard. He currently lives in Sunnyvale with his wife and two kids.
